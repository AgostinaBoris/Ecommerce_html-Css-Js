What to Do on a Romantic Honeymoon in Rome, Italy

Following your nuptials, what could be more romantic than experiencing la dolce vita in Italy’s capital city, where time appears to stand still and cobblestone streets will lead you to awe-inspiring locations both old and new. From its magnificent parks and gardens, to panoramic views overlooking historical buildings, villas, and monuments, to world-renowned ancient sites and architectural landmarks, Rome has it all. It’s no wonder the city has captured the hearts of locals and travelers from across the world for centuries. With direct flights to Rome’s Fiumicino Leonardo da Vinci Airport (FCO) from many major cities in the United States, traveling to this magical place for your honeymoon is relatively easy. Once you arrive, simply take the Leonardo Express train to the Termini Station nearby many hotels in the city centre, or opt for a fixed-fare taxi or private car transport to your hotel. As soon as you step foot in the heart of the Eternal City, explore as you wish. With history at every corner, your itinerary will surely guide you through spectacular squares and architectural sites, palatial monuments and museums, and of course, outstanding Italian wines and cuisine from both fine ristoranti and more-casual trattorias.

How to Choose Flowers for Your Wedding Date & Season

In addition to great food, drinks, and entertainment, most couples choose to focus the majority of the spending limit for their wedding on stunning florals throughout the festivities. A spectacular flower design has the ability to transform a space into a romantic, fragrant, and magical setting that will set the tone for the special celebration and wow brides, grooms, and their guests. Depending on the style, location, and aesthetic of your nuptials, an expert florist and event designer will offer recommendations to illustrate your wedding vision in floral form. They’ll also take into account your individual wedding date and the season you’ll say “I do” to find the perfect blossoms that are available around the world to fit your desired look.

Who We Are

i there! Welcome to Brides, where every couple, every wedding, and every love is celebrated. Whether you are a soon-to-be-wed, newlywed, wedding party member, or guest, we are so excited to be on this journey with you. A bit about us: We’ve been around for 87 years and counting, making us the most seasoned wedding publication in the United States. However, we didn’t always carry the name Brides. When we printed our first pages in 1934, the title was So You’re Going to Be Married. About 25 years later, that turned into The Bride’s Magazine, which was then shortened to Bride’s. Eventually, we dropped the apostrophe and said “I do” to the name we all know and love today: Brides. At Brides, we also believe there is no better way to find wedding inspiration than to look at how real couples tie the knot. That’s why we regularly highlight the love stories and nuptials of couples across the globe through our Real Weddings. With features like this boho beach wedding featuring a flash mob, this traditional Indian celebration with modern touches, or this elegant Nantucket affair with a martini tower, you’ll find countless photos, ideas, and tried-and-true advice from newlyweds in every story. Interested in submitting your own Real Wedding? Click here to learn how. But enough about us: Your wedding is all about you and your true love. We can’t wait to guide you to the big day and provide endless advice and inspiration along the way. Now let’s get you married!
